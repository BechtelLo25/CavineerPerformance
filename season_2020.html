Cavineers's 2020 season

Robot

While this season was cancelled mid way through due to covid-19 the robot Kronk was still produced. Kronk was challenged to pick up power cells and shoot them into goals of 2 diffrent heigts and also had to climb at the end of the match. This robot consisted of an intake for picking up power cells, a shooter for shooting them into high and low goals, a climber, and a tank drive.
